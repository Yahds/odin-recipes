Mini Pancake Parfait

Description

A Mini Pancake Parfait is a charming and delectable dessert that combines the classic appeal of pancakes with the delightful layers of a parfait. This dessert is not only visually appealing but also offers a delightful medley of flavors and textures.

It is a visually appealing dessert that captures the essence of a classic breakfast while delivering an array of delightful flavors. Mini Pancake Parfaits are perfect for brunches, special occasions, or as a creative and fun dessert option.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon jam or fruit preserves
  • 5 (mini) Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 teaspoon pure maple syrup, or to taste

Steps

  1. In a wide-mouthed glass or jar, layer 1/3 cup vanilla yogurt.
  2. Top with 1 tablespoon jam or fruit preserves.
  3. Add 3 mini pancakes
  4. Place 1/2 cup mixed berries
  5. Repeat yogurt and berry layers.
  6. Add 2 additional pancakes and drizzle with maple syrup.
